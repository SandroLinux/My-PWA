Amazon has announced Halo

Amazon has announced Halo which is a fitness band with no screen which Amazon claims using your camera will be able to create 3D scans for body fat and will be able to listen for the emotion in your voice. The band will cost will cost $99.99 and a $3.99 per month subscription to unlock advanced functionality.
